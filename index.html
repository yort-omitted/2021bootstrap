  • Improved blood flow
  • Improved heart and lung health
  • Improved mental health
  • Stress relief
  • Weight loss

Form

Proper form is always something to have on your mind while working out. Form gives you strength without strain!

Proper form can help you avoid injuries and improve overall preformance. Form can help you work smarter, not harder as it will hone your focus on the muscles you are trying to engage. If you do not take form into account, you could cause strains and tears in the tendons and muscles.

Three things to consider on figuring out proper form and technique:

  • Alignment
  • Range of Motion
  • Tension

Diet

Physical exercise is one thing but diet is what puts everything together!

You need to make sure your body is getting the right nutritions, energy, and many other neccessities. When you are exercising, you need to make sure you are getting all of your nutritional requirements. The best energy is glucose which your body extracts from carbohydrates.

If you are focusing on developing your muscles, protein will be the number one nutrition to focus on. Consuming protein from a protein bar or protein shakes during and after your workout can help repair and build more muscle. Protein is a another source of energy that could boost your overall performace while exercising.
